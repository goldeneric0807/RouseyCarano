Place your fighter images in this folder:

  rousey.jpg   — Photo of Ronda Rousey (full body or upper body, portrait orientation)
  carano.jpg   — Photo of Gina Carano (full body or upper body, portrait orientation)

Tips for best results:
- Use portrait/vertical images (taller than wide)
- Images with transparent or dark backgrounds look best
- Full-body shots showing fighting stance are ideal
- Good free sources: Google Images, Getty Images (for personal use), AP Images

The site will automatically fall back to the SVG silhouettes if the images are missing.
